### The Instrument: MiroFish

MiroFish is a swarm intelligence engine. You feed it source material — in this case, the complete Oro Verde Reality Seed for Season 1 — and it does the following:

It extracts every character, every psychological profile, every relationship, every secret, every information asymmetry, and every worldbuilding rule from that document. It builds a knowledge graph from all of it. Then it populates that graph with autonomous AI agents who carry those psychologies, those wounds, those starting positions — and it releases them into a world.

That world has two social environments running simultaneously:

- **INFO PLAZA** — the public-facing platform. Think Twitter. Corporate statements, press positioning, public performance, journalistic output.
- **TOPIC COMMUNITY** — the private, semi-anonymous platform. Think Reddit. Unfiltered opinion, fear, conspiracy, confession, rumor.

The agents were not given a plot. They were given who they are — and left to generate what happens next.

The simulation ran **168 rounds**. That is one full television season: approximately 10 episodes at 17 rounds per episode. Whatever emerged from those 168 rounds is not conjecture. It is the organic output of 20+ characters with distinct psychologies acting in accordance with who they are.
